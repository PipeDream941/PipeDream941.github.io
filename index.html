Zhong Siyao

Self-Introduciotn

I was born in Lanzhou, Gansu Province, China, which is famous for the Yellow River, mutton and beef noodles.

I studied at the University of International Business and Economics in Beijing as an undergraduate, majoring in e-commerce and minoring in finance.

In college, I participated in debate teams, and joined entrepreneurship counseling centers. Here I met good friends and learned about some startups.

Meanwhile, I and my classmates often volunteered and we created a hospice program to take care for children who are seriously ill.

Because I was exposed to programming courses at university, I was very interested in computers, so I applied for IT at the graduate level.

Hobby

billiards

After graduating from university, I was introduced to billiards on the recommendation of my friends, and I was fascinated by this sport and studied for about three months.
Although I have not entered the billiard hall for a long time due to the covid19, but I will continue to study in the future.

Game

I do love to play games. Games have been a part of my life since my father bought me my first PSP in elementary school. As for game types, compared to PVP games like LOL and CSGO that most players like, I prefer single player games and independent game. The game I spent the most time on was Age of Empires, a RTS game produced by Microsoft. My favorite types of games are ACT, ARPG, AVG, etc
